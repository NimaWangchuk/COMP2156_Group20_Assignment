George Brown College, located in Toronto, Canada, offers a variety of post-secondary programs in fields such as health sciences, business, technology, and arts. Known for its industry partnerships and hands-on learning approach, the college equips students with practical skills for successful careers in a rapidly changing workforce.

 In 2021, George Brown College had 27,128 full-time students, with international students comprising 29% of the student body. The college also had 3,123 part-time students and 58,119 continuing education registrants. 
The college is committed to sustainability and innovation. A notable example is the construction of Limberlost Place, a 10-storey mass-timber building set to open in 2024.
