###About George Brown College
George Brown College is a prominent public college located in downtown Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Founded in 1967, the college has grown into one of the largest and most diverse educational institutions in Canada. The college offers more than 160 full-time programs and 200 continuing education certificates across three main campuses: Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront. George Brown College is known for its strong industry connections, hands-on learning approach, and exceptional career-focused education in areas including business, hospitality and culinary arts, engineering technology, health sciences, and creative arts.


The college serves over 30,000 full-time students annually, including a large international student population from more than 100 countries. George Brown takes pride in its high graduate employment rate and strong relationships with industry partners, ensuring students receive practical, real-world experience alongside their academic studies.



Notable features include state-of-the-art facilities, such as the Chef School, one of North America's leading culinary schools, and the Waterfront Campus, which houses the Centre for Health Sciences and the Daphne Cockwell Centre for Health Sciences.
